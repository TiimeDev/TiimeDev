Frequently Asked Questions

What kind of businesses do you work with?

We specialize in helping local businesses of all sizes modernize their online presence. This includes restaurants, retail stores, service providers (like dentists, lawyers, contractors), and more. If you have an outdated website or no website at all, we can help!

How long does a website redesign take?

The timeline for a website redesign can vary depending on the complexity of the project, the number of pages, and the features required. A basic starter site might take 2-4 weeks, while a more complex business growth package could take 4-8 weeks. We'll provide a detailed timeline after our initial consultation.

Do you provide ongoing website maintenance?

Yes, we offer monthly website maintenance packages to ensure your site remains secure, up-to-date, and performs optimally. This includes software updates, security monitoring, backups, and content update assistance. Details can be discussed based on your needs. We also offer more advanced monthly add-ons like SEO and E-commerce management.

Can you help with Search Engine Optimization (SEO)?

Absolutely! Basic on-page SEO setup is included in all our packages. For more comprehensive SEO strategies, including local SEO and ongoing optimization, we offer specialized services to help improve your search engine rankings and attract more organic traffic. Check out our "SEO & Content Growth" monthly add-on for more details.

What's included in the "Free Website Audit"?

Our free website audit involves a comprehensive review of your current website (if you have one). We'll look at design, user experience, mobile-friendliness, loading speed, basic SEO, and identify areas for improvement. We'll then provide you with a summary of our findings and recommendations, with no obligation.
